UniBake Fostering Social Connections Among University Students in Residential Halls Using a Dynamic Network Model Dependent on Culinary Interest

Abstract: University Students in residential halls, particularly first-year halls, regularly foster social connections by cooking together. However, the participants involved is determined by proximity. We are proposing the implementation of a dynamic network model dependent on culinary interest.
